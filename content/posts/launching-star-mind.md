Started another side project this week — [Star Mind](https://www.star-mind.top).

Dusted off an old domain (`star-mind.top`) and turned it into a review site for `free SEO and marketing tools`. No affiliate fluff, just me actually testing the free tiers and writing down where they break.

![Star Mind Screenshot](/images/star-mind-screenshot.png)

## **Why?**

While working on [MindGift](https://www.getmindgift.com), I realized there's always a few weeks between shipping and seeing any real traction. I used to just stare at analytics and tweak copy. Not productive.

So this time I thought: why not just start another thing while I wait?

Also googled around — turns out people are searching for “`free SEO tools`” and “`SERP checker`” quite a bit. Figured I'd give it a shot.

![free-seo-tools-search-trends](/images/free-seo-trends.png)


## **What's on the site?**

First version is simple:

- Tell people exactly where the free plan hits a limit
- Actually run real tasks, screenshot the annoying parts
- No star ratings — just “this is for who”

First review is up: WhatsMySerp, a rank checker. More coming.

## **Next**

Gonna work through SEO tools first, then maybe expand to design / productivity stuff.

There's also a tiny trends chart at the bottom. Just playing around with it. If it's useful, might turn it into a weekly thing.

---

Small bet. Might work, might not. At least I didn't sit around waiting.
